Buildings Guide

Explore your campus by clicking on any building on the map. Each building will show you:

  • Building name and information
  • Number of floors and classrooms
  • Access to classroom details and locations

Classrooms Guide

Finding classrooms is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. 1 Click on a building on the map
  2. 2 Select the floor you need
  3. 3 View all classrooms and rooms on that floor

You can also use the search bar to find specific classrooms quickly!
