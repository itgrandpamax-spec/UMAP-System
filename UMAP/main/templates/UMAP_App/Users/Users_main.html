Emergency Guide

Evacuation & Safety Info

In Case of Emergency

Follow campus security instructions immediately. Call 911 or campus security. Proceed to your evacuation zone.

Evacuation Zones

Legend: Each building has a color-coded zone. Evacuate to the zone designated by color.

Stay Safe

  • • Move quickly
  • • No elevators
  • • Stay calm

Do Not

  • • Re-enter building
  • • Leave zone
  • • Ignore alerts

Fire Exits

Orange marked exits. Know 2 routes minimum.

Assembly Point

Wait in your zone. Account for everyone/friends or anything suitable or section.

Emergency Contacts

Life-threatening: 911
Campus Security: Always Call out for nearest Guards
Non-emergency: You may reach out to your respective Professor or Campus Office.
